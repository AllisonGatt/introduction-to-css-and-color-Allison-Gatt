About Me

Allison Gatt

My name is Allison Gatt and I am a senior at Madonna majoring in Biology and double minoring in Chemistry and Computer Science. I hope to graduate May 2025.

One of my favorite hobbies is paddle boarding, and there are several reasons why I enjoy it so much. First, it allows me to fully immerse myself in the outdoors, offering a sense of adventure and excitement as I explore. While I love being near the water, I do not always love to be actively swimming, which makes paddle boarding perfect since I can enjoy the water without having to be in it the entire time. Plus, it is a fantastic way to stay active and get a great workout.
